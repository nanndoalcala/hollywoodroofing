About Hollywood Roofing

A Master Roofer.
A Family Man.
A Man of Faith.

Gregory Gonzales has been roofing for 43 years — carrying on a tradition that spans 3 generations. At 64 years old, Greg brings a lifetime of expertise to every roof he touches, with a goal to retire at 80.

Greg is a proud father of 3 — two boys (ages 38 and 36) and a daughter (30). His daughter, her husband, and his 3 beautiful granddaughters live with him, along with his 96-year-old father. Family is everything.

Faith changed Greg's life. With God's direction, he dreams of starting a foundation for cancer patients and neglected kids — using his success in roofing to give back to those who need it most.
